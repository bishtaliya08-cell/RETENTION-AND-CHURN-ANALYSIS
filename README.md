Telecom Retention & Churn Analysis
📊 Overview

Customer retention and churn are critical indicators of business sustainability and long-term profitability. Since customers are the core of any organization, understanding their behavior is essential for driving growth and maintaining a competitive edge.

This project focuses on analyzing telecom data to identify patterns and key factors influencing customer churn and retention.

🎯 Objective

The primary goal of this analysis is to:

Identify factors affecting customer churn
Understand customer behavior and retention patterns
Provide data-driven insights for better decision-making
Highlight areas where the business may be underperforming
